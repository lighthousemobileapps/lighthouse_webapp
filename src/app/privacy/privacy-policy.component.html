By using LightHouse, you agree to the collection, use, and disclosure of your personal information as described in this Privacy Policy.

What Information We Collect


LightHouse collects various types of personal information from you, including:

Personal data: When you create an account or use certain features of LightHouse, we may collect your personal data, such as your name, age, gender, and vehicle information.

We use your personal information for the following purposes:

To provide and improve LightHouse: We use your information to provide and improve LightHouse, including:

To personalize your experience: We use your information to personalize your experience with LightHouse, including:

Recommending videos and features that may be of interest to you

To comply with laws and regulations: We may use your information to comply with applicable laws and regulations, such as those related to data security and privacy.

To protect our rights and interests: We may use your information to protect our rights and interests, such as detecting and preventing fraud.

How We Share Your Information


We may share your personal information with the following third parties:

Business partners: We may share your information with our business partners who offer products or services that may be of interest to you.

Law enforcement and other government agencies: We may disclose your information to law enforcement or other government agencies if required by law or if we believe disclosure is necessary to protect our rights or interests or to comply with a legal process.

You have the following choices regarding your personal information:

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, or as required by law or regulation. We regularly review our data retention practices and delete or anonymize personal information that is no longer needed.
